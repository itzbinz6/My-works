About Kai

Twelve years.
One frame at a time.

I picked up my first film camera at seventeen and never really put it down. What started as a way to slow down became a craft, then a career, then something closer to a calling.

I work primarily with 35mm and medium format, shooting portraits, weddings, editorial, and documentary work. Based in Lagos, available worldwide.
