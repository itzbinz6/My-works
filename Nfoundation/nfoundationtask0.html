Think global, act local

NayePankh started during the toughest months of the pandemic, when a handful of college students began sharing whatever little they had with people who had even less. What began as a few packed meals grew into one of India's largest student-led NGOs — registered with the UP Government and certified under 80G & 12A.

We're still run the same way we started: directly, locally, and by people young enough to believe it's still possible to fix things. No middlemen, no red tape — just students who show up.

What we do

Five ways we show up

Every program is run on the ground by our own student teams — not outsourced, not abstract.

Food security
Daily meal drives for street communities and families who go without — and food left out for the strays nobody else feeds.
Hygiene & sanitary care
Free sanitary products and awareness drives that break the silence around menstrual health.
Education access
Books, uniforms and tutoring, so a child's schooling never depends on a family's worst month.
Clothing drives
Warm clothes and daily essentials handed directly to families during winter and crisis.
Animal welfare
Feeding and caring for stray animals alongside every community drive we run.

Good to ask

Frequently asked questions

Is my donation tax-exempt?

Yes. NayePankh Foundation is certified under 80G of the Indian Income Tax Act, so your donation qualifies for up to 50% tax relief. You'll receive a receipt for every contribution.

How do I know my donation reaches the right people?

Every rupee goes directly into food, hygiene kits, school supplies and clothing distributed by our own on-ground student teams — no middlemen. We regularly post drive updates on our social channels.

Can I intern or volunteer without prior NGO experience?

No experience necessary, just willingness to show up. Every new intern goes through a short orientation with our team before joining a drive.

Is NayePankh Foundation officially registered?

Yes. We're registered under the Indian Societies Act, 1860, recognized by the UP Government, affiliated with Niti Aayog, and certified under both 80G and 12A.
