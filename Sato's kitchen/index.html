Our Story

Women-owned. Community-rooted. Brixton-built.

Sato's Kitchen opened on Stockwell Road with one goal: cook the food West Africa has always eaten and serve it to the neighbourhood that deserves it. No shortcuts. Just the recipes that have travelled across generations.

Every bowl of egusi, every plate of pounded yam, every grilled fish comes out of a kitchen where authenticity is non-negotiable. Our guests leave full — and they always come back.

LGBTQ+ friendly, family-welcoming, and proud of it. Everyone eats here.
