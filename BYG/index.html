Our Story

Born in the backyard. Built for Lagos.

The Backyard Grill Ikeja started as a simple love for fire and food — and turned into one of Allen's most-loved spots. We do things the old way: real charcoal, real smoke, real seasoning. No shortcuts.

Every plate that leaves our kitchen carries the same energy — bold, smoky, and packed with flavour. Whether it's a solo lunch or a big spread with the crew, BYG is where Lagos eats well.
