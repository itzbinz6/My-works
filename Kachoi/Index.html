Our Story

A bukka spot built for real hunger

Kachi's Kitchen started with one simple idea: Lagos deserved a bukka spot that actually tasted like home. Not watered-down stews. Not tiny portions for big prices. Just proper Nigerian food — the kind that has you licking the plate and wondering when you can come back.

Tucked inside Twenty Sixth and Park Avenue in Lekki Phase I, we serve dine-in, drive-through, and no-contact delivery — so whether you're rushing back to the office or settling in with the family, we've got you covered.

🔥 Cooked Fresh Every pot is made fresh daily. Nothing sits overnight.
🌿 Local Ingredients We source from trusted local suppliers and markets.
🏠 Home Recipes Real Nigerian recipes, not hotel adaptations.
💛 Cozy & Clean A beautiful, relaxed space to eat and unwind.

What We Serve

Straight from the pot

Every dish is cooked fresh daily with proper Nigerian ingredients. This isn't a menu — it's a mood.

🍚
Rice Dishes
Party Jollof Rice

The smoky, smoky kind — cooked low and slow over firewood the way your aunty used to make it at owambes. Served with your choice of protein.

🥣
Soups & Stews
Egusi Soup

Rich, thick egusi loaded with assorted meat and stockfish. Best eaten with pounded yam that gives you that stretch. Order of satisfaction guaranteed.

🍖
Grills & Proteins
Suya & Peri-Peri Chicken

Marinated in house-blended spice rubs and grilled to perfection. Pair with our pepper sauce for the full experience.

🫕
Classics
Buka Stew & Swallow

The original combo that never fails — our signature buka stew served with eba, semo, or pounded yam. Simple, filling, deeply satisfying.

🍲
Pepper Soups
Catfish Pepper Soup

A proper pepper soup that clears your sinuses and warms your soul. Made with point-and-kill catfish and a blend of seven Naija spices.

🥤
Drinks & Sides
Zobo, Kunu & Cold Drinks

House-made zobo with ginger and cloves, creamy kunu aya, and cold drinks to wash it all down. The perfect ending to a proper meal.

FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

What time do you open?
We open daily from 8am. Last orders are typically taken before close — follow us on Instagram (@kachis.kitchen_) for daily updates on when we're set and what's available that day.
How do I place an order for delivery or pickup?
You can DM us on WhatsApp or Snapchat (@kachiskitchen) — we alternate between both channels and respond quickly. You can also order via Glovo for delivery, or simply walk in for dine-in or drive-through.
Do you cater for events or large orders?
Yes, we do! We handle corporate lunches, family gatherings, owambes, and other events. Reach out via WhatsApp or Snapchat at least 48 hours in advance so we can plan properly and make sure everything is fresh and on point.
Where exactly are you located?
We're at 1E Babatope Bejide Crescent, Lekki Phase I, Lagos — inside the Twenty Sixth and Park Avenue complex. We're easy to find; just search "Kachi's Kitchen Lekki" on Google Maps for directions.
Is the food halal?
We use locally sourced meats and cook with traditional Nigerian methods. For specific dietary or halal questions, please reach out to us directly on WhatsApp or Snapchat before your order and we'll give you a clear answer.
How fresh is the food — do you cook daily?
Every single day. We start cooking fresh each morning and whatever hasn't sold by closing time does not get carried over. That's our promise to you — if it's on our counter, it was made today.
