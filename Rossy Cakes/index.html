Our Story

Baked with
precision & heart

Rossycakes & Events is Ilorin's trusted cake studio, operating from No. 15 Ambassador Street, Gaa Akanbi. Every cake that leaves our kitchen is the result of careful craftsmanship — from the first sketch to the final flourish.

Whether you walk in for a birthday cake or call us for a full wedding display, you get the same commitment: beautiful design, quality ingredients, and honest care.
