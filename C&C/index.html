Our Story

A homey
little spot
in Plano

Cup & Cup started with a simple belief — that healthy drinks could taste extraordinary. Owner Sarah Han brings traditional Korean recipes to Plano, made from scratch every day using Japanese and New York imported ingredients.

What makes us different isn't just the menu. It's the feeling. Walk in and you'll smell something like cookies. Sarah will make you feel right at home.
