How Pulse works

1
Drop in your context

Paste notes, data, or a rough outline. Pulse reads what you have and understands what you're trying to say — not just what you typed.

2
Pulse drafts in your voice

It learns your team's tone, structure preferences, and document standards. Every output sounds like you — because it's trained on how you actually write.

3
Review and ship it

You stay in control. Pulse flags weak sections, suggests tighter phrasing, and formats everything to match your template. One click to finalize.
